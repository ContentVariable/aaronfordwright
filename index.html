About Me

Aaron is a B.A. graduate from VCU, who had a major focus in design and theatre carpentry. While his degree initially led him to develop skills in production creation, he learned much more from the hobbies he developed while at college and over the previous years since graduating. During his time at VCU, Aaron spent a lot of time at their aptly named “The Workshop” center, where he recorded his own podcast and learned basic graphic design. In the years since, he has taught himself JavaScript and HTML/CSS, and hopes to be CompTIA certified within the next 3 months. IT work is an extreme passion of his, that while it only sort of flourished during college, definitely came to bloom when he found the time to really dig into the latent fire I had for it since leaving.

Aaron’s biggest strength is his firm belief in really listening to people and correctly assessing their situation. Oversight in what someone may be asking for assistance for may lead to more issues than there had been before. It is important to pay attention to things that may even be beyond the scope of what your employee, manager, or customer may be inquiring about. This has helped him countless times as he has been able to properly solve a problem that may have otherwise been handled ineffectively. A common occurrence while in his productions at VCU, that was mitigated by quick collective group thinking.

Currently, Aaron is progressing his expertise by learning new languages and expanding upon that of which he already knows. He plans on progressing through Node.js, React, and Python by the end of the year. With these languages and front-end knowledge in possession, Aaron has a goal of creating numerous beautiful websites with seamless controls that work for every user. And as for the future, he plans on eventually moving into software design for more in-depth programming.
